Coaching, Consulting, and Education

A collection of resources, presentations, and educational assessments on a range of topics related to library systems and technology as complied, created and curated by Jeremy Nelson. This site is the home for intro2libsys.info LLC, his library systems consulting and programming firm.

History of this site

This site initially started off as an online text-book for a graduate course taught at the University of Denver's Library and Information Science program.
