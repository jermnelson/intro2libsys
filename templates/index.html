History of this site

This site initially started off as an online text-book for a graduate course taught at the University of Denver's Library and Information Science program.

Now this site is collection of online tutorials and references topics on Libraries and Library Systems. We are the home of the catalog pull platform, an open-source collection of bibliographic services. provide opportunities for individuals to demostrate their compentency and earn their open badges on most of these topics. Many of these badges are free to take and earn your badge, some badges have a minimial charge.

Coaching!?

Technology should be easy enough that you only need coaching to use it better, not be trained in its use.
