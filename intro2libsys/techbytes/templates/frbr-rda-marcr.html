FRBR

Functional Requirements for Bibliographic Records ( FRBR) is an entity-relationship model where the objects of interest, called entities, are defined in a most general way as possible along with the relationships between those entities. FRBR's final report was written in 1998

WEMI Entities

  • Work
  • Expression
  • Manifestation
  • Item

RDA

Resource Description and Access is the new cataloging standard to replace AACR2. RDA. RDA provides guidelines for cataloging digital objects with a focus on helping users find, identify, select, and optain information.

Source http://igelu.org/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/IGeLU-sally-McCallum.pptx.

MARCR

The Library of Congress Bibliographic Framework Initiative recently offered a status update of their bibliographic modeling work to replace MARC21 with a new model they call MARCR for MARC Resources.

MARCR uses concepts from Linked Data while simplying elements from FRBR and RDA
