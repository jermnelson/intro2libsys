Entrepreneurs are Everywhere

As library leaders, look for entrepreneurial or creative ideas from the task and managerial levels in the library.

As library managers, look for technologies and services that can either enhance patron services, improve staff task productivity, or assist in assessment or measurement activities within the next 24 months.

AGILE Software Development Principles

The AGILE model of software development revolves around short, iterative cycles where requirements are generated from direct user interaction and software is developed and tested to meet those specific requirements. These principles can be used in more traditional IT departments in Libraries as software is customized to meet the needs of the library. Software systems is becoming more modular and easier to build connecting services through open web APIs and open-source systems.

What is the start-up activity in Library Technology?

In recent years there has been a consolidation of companies in the traditional library bibliographic industry, start-up activity is more active in the digital repository sub-market with such companies as DiscoveryGarden and MediaShelf.

Start-up activity in the Semantic Web is far greater with more opportunities for individuals with a library background or education.
