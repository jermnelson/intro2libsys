=======================================
NoSQL, Mobile Platforms, & Semantic Web
=======================================
Three areas of rapid technological growth and change; NoSQL, mobile 
platforms, and the semantic web, all have important implications and
impacts to library systems. While at a first glance these three 
technological areas may seem distinct; in fact, they complement each other
and depend on each other to deliver the modern software applications and
services that are in wide use today.
