=========================================
Optimizing the Library Systems Department 
=========================================
With Library Systems closely linked to technology, we need to borrow and adapt to changing
requirements, monitary and resource constraints, while offering better service to our libraries.
This chapter presents a techniques, methodologies, and other organizational and management
practices and supporting technology borrowed from best practices from successful software 
projects from open-source, corporation, and start-ups. This approuch approaches organizations 
DevOps Organization.
